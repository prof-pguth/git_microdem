Sentinel-1 Overview:

  • Characteristics
    • C band SAR
    • 12 day repeat cycle, LTAN=1800 hours
    • Data online within 24 hours
  • Interpretation
    • https://ceos.org/ard/files/Laymans_SAR_Interpretation_Guide_2.0.pdf
  • Easiest download from EO Browser (directions)
  • User Guide:  https://sentinels.copernicus.eu/web/sentinel/user-guides/sentinel-1-sar/overview
  • Technical Guide: https://sentinels.copernicus.eu/web/sentinel/technical-guides/sentinel-1-sar
  • Sentinel Hub is good starting place to explore imagery
  • https://asf.alaska.edu/how-to/data-recipes/geocode-sentinel-1-with-gdal/
  • Modes
    • Stripmap (SM) 80 km swath, slightly better than 5x5 m spatial resolution.  Image small islands and in exceptional cases emergency operations
    • Interferometric Wide (IW) swath mode is the main acquisition mode over land and satisfies the majority of service requirements. 250 km swath at 5 m by 20 m spatial resolution, often resampled to 10x10 m or 20x20 m
    • Extra Wide (EW) swath mode  acquires data over a 400 km swath at 20 m by 40 m spatial resolution.
    • Wave mode acquires data in 20 km by 20 km vignettes, at 5 m by 5 m spatial resolution, every 100 km along the orbit, acquired alternately on two different incidence angles.   Wave mode at VV polarisation is the default mode for acquiring data over open ocean.
  • In MICRODEM
    • Need to reproject to UTM before using
    • Open
