DEMIX Tile Requirements

  • Online government portal to download DTMs in Geotiff format.  We now (post version 4 of the DB, allow ASC files)
  • Grid spacing of 2 m or smaller. If the country has several resolutions, such as Switzerland, we use the larger as aggregation to one-arc-second will not benefit from the smaller grid spacing.
  • Vertical datum shifts to EGM2008 from the original datum supported by GDAL. If this is not possible, we can use the DEM only for LSPs which do not depend on absolute elevation values.
  • Availability of tiles approximately 10x10 km, or under 10x10 km that can be merged into tiles of approximately 10x10 km. This rules out huge, multi-GB files. While we could extract tiles from such files, it would not add much to the diversity of test areas, but may remove this requirement in a future version of the database.
  • Elevations in floating point meters with resolution to at least decimeter.
