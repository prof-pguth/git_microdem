DEMIX Analyze Options

The DEMIX options on the main menu are currently working.

  • Modes of difference distributions
  • Histograsm to CSV files
  • Landform categories by data base: database use have AREA and DEMIX_TILE fields, sorted in the order in which you want the AREA plotted.

Others may not be working, and we are working to fix or remove them.

  • DEMIX index high res reference DEMs
  • DEMIX 1 sec reference DEM creation
  • DEMIX reference tile survey
  • DEMIX tiles geostats (disabled)
  • DEMIX elevation histograms
  • Open DEMIX area: create maps (percent difference, air/dirt) and histograms
