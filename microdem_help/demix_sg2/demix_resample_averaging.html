DEMIX Resampling by Averaging

Create reference DEMs for a higher resolution more accurate reference DEM, DTM or DSM or both if available

  • Data Manipulation, Resample, Vertical datum shift, overwrite DEM/grid
  •  Raster GIS menu, Downsampling (creating a lower resolution grid) using the DEMIX 1 sec resample by averaging option. 
    • The ideal high reference DEM will be 1-5 m resolution, but it should also work for a 10 m DEM.
    • Create 3 or more new DEMs
      • 1 sec, pixel-is-area
      • 1 sec, pixel-is-point
      • 1/2 sec (work with both pixel representations)
      • Additional DEMs at high latitudes
