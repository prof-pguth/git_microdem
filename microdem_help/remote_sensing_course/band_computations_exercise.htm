Band Computation Exercise

This will be done in groups of 5.  You will each be assigned to a breakout meet, and assigned a Landsat 8 scene of Yosemite Valley.  The group will prepare a presentation for the entire class at the end of the period.

Data in c:\mapdata\yosemite:

DEM: use Merge_AW30.DEM

Landsat scene (these are subsets of a full scene):

  • Group 1: Companies 1-7, use the subset_1
  • Group 2: Companies 9-16, use the subset_2
  • Group 3: Companies 17-29, use the subset_3

For your assigned scene:

  • Information on the map  Find the position of the sun when the satellite passed overhead, get a matching grayscale shaded reflectance map of the DEM
  • Get histograms of the DNs in the scene, and see if any of the distributions are bimodal.
  • Create new band Get a map showing the brightness temperatures in Band 10.  How will these relate to the true surface temperatures?  Where are the hottest and coldest locations?
  • Create new band showing the radiances and TOA reflectances (with and without the sun angle corrections)  in band 4,5, and 8.  What kinds of values do you get, and how do they vary with band.  Put this in a table.
