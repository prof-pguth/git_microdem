Hyperspectral Imagery Mini-Lab

You can get Hyperion imagery from the Google shared drive, under Hyperion.

Deliverables:

  • Get reflectance spectra for several locations with very different surface materials.
  • Compare the Hyperion spectra with laboratory reference spectra.
  • Compare band scattergrams for correlated and uncorrelated bands.
  • Discuss whether the hyperspectral imagery provides any advantages over Landsat (both 30 m), and what the costs of the hyperspectral imagery are.
  • Discuss what the unsupervised classification shows, and identify some of the clusters.  Comment on the three scattergrams created (e.g. what can you say about the category at the lower left on each scattergram?).
