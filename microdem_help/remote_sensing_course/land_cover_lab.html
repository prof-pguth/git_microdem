Land Cover Lab

Deliverables

  • Compare the ALOS and COP DEMs, with a difference map and visual comparison of the hillshades.  Where are the big differences?
  • Compare the 30 m NLCD and the 100 m Copernicus Land cover.  What are the most significant changes in the Las Vegas area?
  • Include a map of downtown Las Vegas Open Street Map, showing the roads and buildings.  You will have to zoom in quite a bit.

Directions

  • Get all data sets covering Las Vegas, the mountain ranges to the west and north, and all of Lake Mead.
  • Get a 250 m MODIS image covering Las Vegas.
  • Get the ALOS (do not get ellipsoidal) and COP 30 m DEMs for the region around Las Vegas from OpenTopography (Directions)
    • Get a difference map.  Think about the difference value to highlight.
  • Copernicus Global Land Cover 100 m   
    • Download at https://lcviewer.vito.be/download .  Get the 2015 through 2019 tiles that cover Las Vegas.
  • Open as a DEM/grid, US NLCD  from  MLRC, 30 m resolution.  Get all the available years.  In addition to each year, you will have an overall change map.  You can get a popup legend.
  • Open the two DEMs
    • File, Import, GDAL raster  subset to match map to open the Copernicus land cover.  This must be started on the DEM, or another map with a geographic map projection.
