Aspect Difference

Found on Raster GIS, Derivative grids, Aspect

Aspect difference: largest difference in aspect in the NS, NE-SW, EW, or NW-SE directions, with a value 0-180. Gets the aspect from the central point to the 8 neighbors for 3x3, 5x5, and 7x7 windows.   Can help to pick out valleys and ridges
