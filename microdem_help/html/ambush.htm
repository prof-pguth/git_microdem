Ambush/Route Analysis

Accessed via the Calculate, Intervisibility, Route Observation menu choice.

  • Select the route, using either point or stream digitizing.

  • Select the viewshed options.

  • Fans will be drawn (this operation is threaded for multiple cores)

  • Select target area (shapfile) if desired

  • "Edit fans" with  viewshed toolbar

Route analysis along a route shown in red.  Green areas can be seen at some point from the route, and the region shown in blue can be seen at one particular instant from the blue dot overlaid on the route.

Ambush potential along the route.  Points shown in red could see the entire route, making them excellent ambush locations because they have a wide range of times to attack.  Points in purple can see only a few points along the route, making them less effective ambush locations.  White points cannot see the route at all.
