Data spacing for NGA DTED Level 1, which has changes in the E-W spacing at higher latitudes. East-west spacing is show in red, and the nearly constant north-south spacing in green. The validity of the approximation that  DTED Level 1 has 90 or 100 m spacing depends on latitude.

Some of the 1" global DEMs (ALOS and COP) have roughly similar changes in the east-west spacing.  Values for the 1" DEM are 1/3 of those for the 3" DEM.
