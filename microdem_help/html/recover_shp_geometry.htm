Recover Shapefile Geometry

Repair line or area shapefiles if you have the SHP and are missing either SHX or DBF.  The recreated DBF will be empty, so anything that was in it will be gone. 
  • ShapeViewer, http://www.qarah.com/shapeviewer/  Use the "File" menu to open the SHX, and then to create the other two.
  • ShapeChk,
    • http://www.oocities.org/andrew_williamson/useful.html,
    • https://allaroundgis.wordpress.com/2013/12/10/shapefile-shp-shape-checker-v3-2/,
    • https://freegeographytools.com/2008/fixing-broken-shapefiles

You should consider SHZ format to avoid separating the parts of a shapefile.
