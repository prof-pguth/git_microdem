Scatterplot Two Grids

 on Raster GIS menu

  • Can be of the entire grid, or subset area currently on  map.  You will have the value in the first grid on the x axis, and the second grid on the y axis.  If the two grids are not the same size and spacing, you will have the values in the first grid used, and the interpolated value at the same position calculated and plotted on the second axis.
  • Colors will be plot density, since it is very easy to have millions of points with many plotting at the same location on screen, and the focus will be drawn to the large areas with very low density.
  • Graph options lets you change the axes labels and other aspects of the graph.

  • Possible usage:
    • Age of oceanic crust versus depth
    • Age of oceanic crust versus sediment thickness
    • Compare two DEMs covering the same area.
