MERIT DEM

 

Characteristics:

  • Free for education and research.
  • 3" resolution, about 90 m, starting with the SRTM and attempting to deal with voids and other anomalies.
  • The MERIT DEM is referenced to WGS84 and EGM96.
  • Five degree tiles, named for the SW corner
  • Geotiff format.
  • You will have to pass the files through GDAL because of the particular choices in the Geotiff format.
