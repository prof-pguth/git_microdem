Building Density along Coastline

Heat map showing the number of buildings closest to each location along the coastline.
Red line shows regions of the coast that are not the closest point for any buildings.
  • Get Building outlines which will be an area shapefile. 
  • If the building file is large, subset the shapfile to just the area you need.
  • Get coastline, which should be a line shapfile.  Insure the scale is adequate for your purpose.
  • Get a DEM covering the area, with a scale that is adequate
  • Open Grid/DEM
  • Open database (shapefile) for the buildings and the coastline
  • Nearest point on line/perimeter for the centroid of each building
  • Get interpolated elevation for the centroid of each building DEM on Submenu on "EDIT" button on database table form.
  • Filter the buildings, on the elevation and distance from coast, to highlight the desired buildings.
  • Heat map to get the density of buildings along the coast
