Grid Lines--UTM vs Geographic
Two types of grid:
  • UTM: the UTM grid (red, square) runs parallel to the edges of the map.  Older USGS data.
  • Geographic: the lat/long grid (black, quasi-rectangular) runs parallel to the edges of the map.  NGA data and newer USGS data.

These are actual grids, near the edges of UTM grid zones.
