Elevations in Feet

MICRODEM has very little patience with English units. The bulk of the program's operations will use meters. Internally all calculations will be done in meters. DEMs in feet will be correctly handled, with the following caveats:

  • Merged data sets, including samples with elevations in both feet and meters, will be converted to meters. You might consider caution in merging such DEMs as you will lose vertical resolution in the flatter areas.
  • All displays will be labeled in meters.
  • The option to display dual elevations, will show both meters and feet when you roam on the map. This option was enabled to allow comparison of the map display with a paper map with contour lines in feet.

The USGS DEMs in feet offer improved vertical resolution of about 0.3 m, which the program faithfully retains after converting to the metric system.
