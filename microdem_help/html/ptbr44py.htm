Picking From a List Box

The window title bar tells you why you are picking something. The available choices will be in the large list box; pick one by clicking on it. The current choice will be indicated above the list box. When you are satisfied with the selection, choose the OK button.

In some cases you are not allowed to cancel, and must make a selection, because the program is not prepared to clean up in the middle of the operation.
