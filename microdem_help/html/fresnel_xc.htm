Fresnel Zone Cross Sections

Option from LOS Right click

 		  Pick distance along  profile by double clicking on the profile, and get a diagram showing the first and second Fresnel Zones, and the inner 20% of the first Fresnel zone.

If Vegetation grid effects are considered, the vegetation grid will be shown.

If   LIDAR Point clouds is opened, the points will be shown on the profile.
