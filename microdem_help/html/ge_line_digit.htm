Google Earth Line Digitization 

  • Icon on GE toolbar starts digitizing
  • Use either stream or point mode digitizing.
  • When done, save the line by right clicking on it in the Google Earth table of contents.  Use  KML format.
  • To convert to a shapefile, use DNR GPS. 
  • You can also digitize as as sequential points, and then convert them to a line or area shapefile in MICRODEM.
