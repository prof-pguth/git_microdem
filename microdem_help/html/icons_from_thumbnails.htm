Icons From Thumbnails

From Edit,  Icons popup menu on the table display window

  • DB must have an image field.  This is a good use for a DB created from the EXIF field of camera photos.
  • Pick the height desired, and thumbnails will be created. 
  • You can plot these on the map, with additional scaling, so you should err on the side of making a icons a bit larger.
Display with Plot, Icon field

This shows two images.  The GPS locations are off by a few meters, since both pictures were taken on the road.
