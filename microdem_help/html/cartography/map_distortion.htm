Map Distortion

Access this option from the Cartography menu of a map window. The map must be a  Vector Map.

Tissot options

As you roam over the map, a panel on the bottom will show the scale factors h and k (or h' and k') and Omega, the maximum angular distortion. Double clicking will overprint the scale factors at the point, and draw a Tissot indicatrix.

  • h is the scale factor along the meridian
  • k is the scale factor along the parallel
  • If meridians and parallels intersect in right angles, all other scale factors will be between h and k
  • If the projection is conformal, h=k

For some projections:

  • h' is the scale factor along a line radiating from the center of the projection
  • k' is the scale factor along a line perpendicular to a line radiating from the center of the projection
