Open DB and Zoom to its Coverage Area

Often you will open a DB on a map covering a much larger area.  These steps will take you to the area covered by the DB.

  • Database to open the database on the current base map.  To get just the area with the country covered by the DB, either:
    • Plot, Zoom to DB coverage on the toolbar with the database. 
    • Subset & zoom, to the region with your province.  This can take several iterations, depending on how good you are with getting just the desired area.
