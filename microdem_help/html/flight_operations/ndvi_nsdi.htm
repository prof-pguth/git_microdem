NDVI/NSDI

Option on Image analysis menu.

These will create a database and a map; you can do some displays with the database to color by the satellite or the year.

  • NDVI of percentile time series
  • Percentiles of NDVI time series
  • NDVI of percentiles, annual cycle
  • Percentiles of NDVI , annual cycle
  • Point time series
  • Key percentiles

There is a different optionn to create images with NDVI and NDSI
