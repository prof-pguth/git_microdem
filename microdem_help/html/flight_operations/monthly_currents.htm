Monthly Ocean Currents

Map with monthly climate parameters

The data is very dense; you will probably want to subset the map, or thin before plotting vectors.

Data from:

  • http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/phod/dac/dac_meanvel.php currents in ocean, monthly and mean
  • Laurindo, L., A. Mariano, and R. Lumpkin, 2017: An improved near-surface velocity climatology for the global ocean from drifter observations Deep-Sea Res. I, 124, pp.73-92, doi:10.1016/j.dsr.2017.04.009 .
  • Lumpkin, R. and G. C. Johnson, 2013: Global Ocean Surface Velocities from Drifters: Mean, Variance, ENSO Response, and Seasonal Cycle. Geophys. Res.-Oceans,v.118, pp.2992-3006, doi:10.1002/jgrc.20210.  This is the older data set, which was much smaller.

Alternatives:

  • Erik van Sebille, Matthew H England, and Gary Froyland, 2012, Origin, dynamics and evolution of ocean garbage patches from observed surface drifters In Environmental Research Letters, volume 7, number 044040. doi:10.1088/1748-9326/7/4/044040.--has a Matlab transitions matrix
  • http://www.adrift.org.au/ for drift of plastic trash, with option to download a CSV file
