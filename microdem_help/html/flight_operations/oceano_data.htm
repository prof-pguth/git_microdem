Oceanography Data

Built in options

Climate options, Monthly ocean currents.  Data converted from http://www.aoml.noaa.gov/phod/dac/dac_meanvel.php .  

  • Lumpkin, R. and G. C. Johnson, 2013: Global Ocean Surface Velocities from Drifters: Mean, Variance, ENSO Response, and Seasonal Cycle. Geophys. Res.-Oceans, vol. 118, p.2992-3006, doi:10.1002/jgrc.20210.

Recommended sources

  • CTD profile data 
    •  http://www.nodc.noaa.gov/GTSPP/access_data/index.html
  • http://podaac-tools.jpl.nasa.gov/las: Pick parameter (Chlorophyll, Ocean Temperature, Sea Surface Salinity), subset the map, pick the time period.  Use Save As, pick ArcGrid as the format. The file names are not helpful, so keep track of what you get. Might be very slow.  Open as a DEM.

Other potential sources

  • SST or Sea surface temperatures
    • https://climatedataguide.ucar.edu/climate-data/sst-data-hadisst-v11
    • http://marine.rutgers.edu/cool/sat_data/?nothumbs=0&product=sst
    • https://www.ssec.wisc.edu/data/sst/ daily images, which could have a world file added
    • https://agupubs.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/epdf/10.1029/2021GL095590 (Late 2021) lists multiple sources of SST data
  • Ocean currents
    • http://www.oscar.noaa.gov/datadisplay/oscar_datadownload.php   Ocean currents.  Make maps online, or download data in NetCDF format (could not get to work, 1/3/2015).
  • Ocean grids from alitmetry, https://www.aviso.altimetry.fr/en/data/products.html.  NetCDF, which I have not tried to use.
  • Coastal portal
  • http://data.planetos.com/datasets  Marine mammal tracking http://www.gtopp.org/
  • Global shipping lanes:
    • http://sos.noaa.gov/Datasets/dataset.php?id=145  Sampling of 11% of the ships at sea, 2004-2005.  Actual data not there.
  • Buoys
    • http://www.ndbc.noaa.gov/station_page.php?station=tplm2
    • http://www.ndbc.noaa.gov/station_history.php?station=tplm2
  • Pathfinder 4 km SST:
    • ftp://data.nodc.noaa.gov/pub/pathfinder/seasonal_climatology/  Geotiff seasonal climatologies; colored images, not values, broken 8/3/2018
  • Tide records
    • https://gesla787883612.wordpress.com/
