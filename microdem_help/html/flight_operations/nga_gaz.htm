NGA Gazetteer

NGA produces gazetteers for every country in the world except the US (Compare with USGS gazetteer).  There are 252 regions, most of which are countries but there are also territories and other regions like the West Bank, officially "geopolitical areas".

The files use standard 2 digit abbreviations for the region.

Download and convert gazetteer files for use.

 

FC, Feature Class: Nine (9) major feature categories into which similar feature designations are grouped.

  • A = Administrative region 
  • P = Populated place 
  • V = Vegetation 
  • L = Locality or area 
  • U = Undersea 
  • R = Streets, highways, roads, or railroad 
  • T = Hypsographic
  • H = Hydrographic 
  • S = Spot
