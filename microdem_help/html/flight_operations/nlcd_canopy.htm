NLCD Percent Canopy

NLCD canopy as a grid/DEM.

Values range from 0 to 100, and represent the percentage of the 30 m pixel covered by forest canopy.  You can do all operations to display or manipulate this as if it is a grid or DEM.

NLCD canopy as an image.

The Geotiff has a color table associated, and the non-forested areas are gray.  The darker the green colors, the higher the percentage of canopy in the 30 m pixel.

This layer is not related to the vegetation height, or only very indirectly related to vegetation height.
