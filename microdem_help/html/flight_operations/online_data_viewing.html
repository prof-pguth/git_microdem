Online Data Viewing

Satellites

DEMs

  • https://apps.nationalmap.gov/3depdem/
    • View elevation, hillshade, slope, aspect, contours
    • Link to download 1/3 sec Geotiff DEM (1/2 GB per 1x1 degree tile)
    • Export view in web mercator

Land Cover

  • Web viewer Copernicus Landcover from 1992 to 2015: https://openlandmap.org/#/?base=Stamen%20(OpenStreetMap)&center=39.0000,25.0000&zoom=4&opacity=80&layer=lcv_land.cover_esacci.lc.l4_c&time=2015
  • World cover viewer: https://viewer.esa-worldcover.org
  • Ecosystems: https://rmgsc.cr.usgs.gov/wtee/wtee.shtml

Point clouds

  • USGS online viewer for lidar https://usgs.entwine.io/
    • Click on one of the projects on the map, or in the list
    • I prefer the Cesium viewer (zoom in, and see it on the aerial photography), but you can also check Potree
    Heritage scans:
    • https://app.iconem.com/#/map?lat=-1.1368683772161603e-13&lng=6.0000000000007105&sc.order=asc&sc.orderBy=country&z=1.255505941356651
    • https://cyark.org/

Factsheet

  • https://geofolio.org/  Define an area of interest anywhere in the world, and automagically create thematic factsheets containing interactive maps, charts, written overviews, and statistical summaries – all based on open geodata.
