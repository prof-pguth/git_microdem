Oceanography/Marine Mapping in MICRODEM

Some of these options are very specialized, in terms of the data formats required and assumptions made.  The documentation may also be limited, since they are not anticipated as being widely used.  If you want to use them, contact us to see if they meet your needs.

  •   Sidescan sonar image analysis
  •  Subbottom profiler analysis
  • Plot CTD Data
  • Drift models
  • Drifter/buoys
  • AUVs
  • Quantify coastal erosion

Database Table view, Edit, Field arithmetic can compute SigmaTee, SigmaTheta, and Sound Velocity if the fields TEMP_C and SALINITY and DEPTH exist; you can rename fields;  you can then plot profiles with Stats, 2D graph options.

Coastal/Nearshore

  • Coastal beach erosion and time series analysis (e.g. Duck)
  • Tide gauges and global sea level rise
  • Tsunami travel times
