Commercial satellites

Vantar (was Maxar, GeoEye and WorldView satellites)		 Best is 30 cm   Daily or better https://app.skyfi.com/explore/  gives an idea of what is availabe.  Cannot see the hi-res imagery Buy commercial product. 

Planet and Vantar (was Maxar)  have similar but not public sites to browse imagery, and both produce data used by journalists.

Airbus Pleiades Neo (30 cm PAN resolution, 1.2 m 6 MSI bands including deep blue and red edge, with at least two possible detections per day) could be another alternative.  One Atlas is their online platform with imagery and WorldDEM; cheapest package is $5K per year,  Airbus is now commonly  being used by journalists.		 https://content.planet.com/snapshots
