New in  Version 4

  Release 4.0 of TerrbaBase II and build 12.01 of MICRODEM in the summer and fall of 1999.

  • OpenGL 3D views.
  • Graphical selection and loading of DEMs from maps.
  • DEMs merged on the fly when they are opened.
  • GIS-like data manipulation of data base files, including ESRI shape files, using ADO
  • "Ambush" movies showing intervisibility along a selected route
  • Plots grids on two datums on maps
  • Show roaming coordinates on all maps, with two datums, or with MGRS and Lat/Long
  • Improved reading of GEOTIFF  imagery and DEMs
  • Exports DEMs in DTED format

Options that appeared in this version, but are no longer available

  • Pipeline calculations for PAPA variant of the program
  • CastleNet query and display of obstacle data base 
  • Direct output of products into PowerPoint
