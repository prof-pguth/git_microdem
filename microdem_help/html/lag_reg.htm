Lag Region Size

Choice on Raster GIS menu, and then "Region size button"

  • Shows the effect of 5 region sizes at selected locations.  Numbers are the region size in meters (full size in meters), best shift in the x direction in meters, best shift in the y direction in meters, and best correlation coeffieicnt.
  • Smaller region is faster, but may not have any diagnostic features.
