Remote Sensing Outline

Learning about GIS and remote sensing involves a mixture of education and training:

  • Education in basic principles which would apply to any GIS or remote sensing software.
    • Electromagnetic spectrum
    • Data characteristics, including scale
    • Data availability
    • Operations with geographic data
  • Training in the specifics of a particular software package, which will not apply directly to other software, or even to the next release of the same software package, but is required to actually use data and understand GIS operations.  If you understand the principles, you can adapt to different software.
