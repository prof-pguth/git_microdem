Several options:

  • Right click on topographic profile; insure profile was correctly oriented to capture periodic structure (perpendicular to crests and troughs).  Adjust Parameters.
  • From Fabric at point option on  Calculate,  Topographic Grain menu.  This calculates the direction of the fabric, draws a profile at right angles, and computes wavelength and height.

Option on LOS Right click:

  • Draw a profile, perpendicular to the dune crests.
    • Peaks are determined by  parameters on Wavelength tab of Topographic fabric vector overlay form
  • Pick option after LOS Right click
  • For each point identified as a peak on the profile, the algorithm will find all points in both directions on the same crest
    • Spacing of points along the crest is currently hard-wired into the program at 500 m.
  • The data will be in a series of CSV files in the temp directory, which will be collected into a single database..
