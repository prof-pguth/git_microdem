Horizon Options

 

Horizon calculation settings 

  • Max Horizon: maximum distance to go out in the DEM to find blocking terrain. The DEM must extend this far from the point or the search will be truncated at the edge of the DEM.
  • Observer above ground (m): how high up is the observer.
  • Radial precision: radials will be drawn with this spacing in meters along the radial. Smaller numbers will give greater accuracy and take longer.
  • Angular precision: distance between radials used to compute the horizon.

Sunlight computations 

  • First day sunlight: starting Julian date for computations.
  • Last day sunlight: ending Julian date for computations.
  • Interval (days)
  • Sunlight precision: minute stepping factor for determining sun position.
  • Single Julian Day: for computation
