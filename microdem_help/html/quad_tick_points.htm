Quad Tick Points

On right click menu from a map.

Right click on a point in the quad where you want a database with the coordinates of the tick marks on the map.

Enter:

  • Size of the map in minutes.  If the map is "rectangular" (e.g. 1x2 degrees), enter the larger size
  • Tick size in minutes.
  • If you want the ticks for NAD27.

Resulting table:

  • LAT,  LONG inWGS4/NAD83
  • X_UTM,Y_UTM in in WGS 84/NAD83
  • LAT_NAD27 and LONG_NAD27 if you asked for them

Use in registering maps on the NAD27 datum when you want to use the ticks.  You can do this in MICRODEM or Google Earth.

To get a portion of the quad, filter the database.
