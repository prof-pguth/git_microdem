Buttons on the map toolbar work with a database:

  •   load the database.
  • ID: identify records in the database. Double clicking on a record will bring up a table with its attributes.
  • focal mechanism, only for earthquakes from the CMT project. Double click on an epicenter, and the beach ball will be drawn, and you can drag it to the desired location.
