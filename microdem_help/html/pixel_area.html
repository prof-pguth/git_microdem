Pixel Area

Option on the Raster GIS, Make derivative grids, slope which creates three grids:

  • Slope factor to increase area (1 / cos(Slope))
  • 2D slope, the product of the pixel size in x and y
  • 3D slope, the 2d slope multiplied by the slope factor

References, using a more complicated triangle method

  • Bian, J., Wang, Y., Zhao, J. et al. TSA-MC v1.0: A 30-m dataset of true surface area for China’s mountains to support SDG 15.4 indicators monitoring. Sci Data 13, 626 (2026). https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-026-06880-6    Used ASTER GDEM, and reprojected to equal area projection.
  • Jenness, J.S. (2004), Calculating landscape surface area from digital elevation models. Wildlife Society Bulletin, 32: 829-839. https://doi.org/10.2193/0091-7648(2004)032[0829:CLSAFD]2.0.CO;2
