Climograph

You can get the climograph

  • Double clicking on record (station) in data base, and picking Record display
  • Record display can rapidly cycle through a number of climographs.
  • Right click on map, Physical geography
  • Climograph--(or choice on the Calculate menu) after selecting this choice, double clicking on a station will bring up its climograph.
A climograph shows temperature in red, with a scale on the left, and  precipitation in blue with a scale on the right.
Gray lines show critical temperatures used in Koppen Classification.
 

Options set on Geology/Geography tab of  Options  form determine how climograph creation works.
