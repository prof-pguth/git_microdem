Citing MICRODEM

The best way to cite MICODEM: 

  • Guth, P.L., 2009, Geomorphometry in MICRODEM, In Hengl, T., Reuter, H.I. (eds), Geomorphometry: concepts, software, applications. Developments in Soil Science Series, Elsevier, ISBN-13: 978-0-12-374345-9, p.351-366.
    •   https://doi.org/10.1016/S0166-2481(08)00015-9

If you would like a journal reference, the best (although probably out of date in details) is probably:

  • Guth, P.L., 1995, Slope and aspect calculations on gridded digital elevation models: Examples from a geomorphometric toolbox for personal computers: Zeitschrift fur Geomorphologie N.F. Supplementband 101, pp.31-52.

Cite the open source code:

  • Guth, P.L., 2023c. GIT-MICRODEM [Delphi source code, archived installation versions]. URL: https://github.com/prof-pguth/git_microdem 

Cite the exectubable and help file downloads

  • Guth, P.L., 2023d, MICRODEM: Open-source GIS with a focus on Geomorphometry [download latest Win64 executable and CHM help file] URL: https://microdem.org/
