MICRODEM Forum

Support for MICRODEM is provided via an online forum at Delphi Forums. This is a private site totally unrelated to the the Naval Academy. There will always be a current link to the forums at the Microdem home page.

This forum will be moderated, and inappropriate comments removed. We may also edit comments, and remove comments that do not provide helpful information. Our goal is to have a useful reference for users to find the answers to questions and share techniques.

We reserve the right to redirect email questions to the forum, where other users can benefit from the discussion.
