MICRODEM's Lists of GIS Data

These links are subject to the vagaries of the web, and could be changed at any time.

Maintenance of these lists is not a primary focus, but I try to add new sites when I run across them that could be of interest to a wide range of students.

When there is a broken link, the data may still be on that site, but you will have to dig around to try to find it.  Perhaps try a google search with the name of the site owner and the type of data you are looking for.

Many sites have http and not https

  • This means that the link was added probably a long time ago
  • Many of the sites will automatically shift you to https
  • Some will likely be broken
  • Be aware of your comfort level with potential security issues
