Map (will be very slow): for every point on the map this computes the results of radio line of sight.  This may or may not be any better than a viewshed; you must really want to see the reasons for LOS blockage to wait for this to compute.  It will color by losIsVisible, losBlockByTerrain, losEdgeFresnelIntrusion, losCenterFresnelIntrusion.  Consider carefully the map size (area and resolution), and running this overnight.

Databases are stored in c:\mapdata\temp directory, and will be deleted on closing the program unless you save them.
