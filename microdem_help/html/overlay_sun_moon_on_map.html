Overlay Sun/Moon Position on the Map

 

Right click on map

The diagram is on the clipboard, and you can move it along the map.  At the each hour the sun or moon will be on the red line and you can see where you must be to get it in a direct line for photography by moving the diagram to line up the line for the hour you want.   • Panorama view
