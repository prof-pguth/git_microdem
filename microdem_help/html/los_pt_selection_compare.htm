Point selection along intervisiblity  lines

See effects of horizontal earth curvature algorithm.  Picked on Calculate, Intervisibility menu from a map.

This will show the difference between three methods of picking the straight line between two points:

  • UTM
  • Lat-Long
  • Geodetic (Vincenty)

You pick a line of sight, and then the number of points along it.  The program will produce a table with the distances between the points, and show them on the map.  There will be little difference until the profile becomes long; the largest differences will be in the middle of the profile.
