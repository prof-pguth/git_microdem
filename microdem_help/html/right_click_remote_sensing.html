Right Click Remote Sensing Options

  • Satellite DNs at points.  For Landsat, it will include radiance, reflectance, and brightness temperature in the thermal bands.
  • TLS (temperature of the land surface) for a range of emissivities, using the TIR bands of Landsat.  While these are reported with 30 m pixels, the actual resolution is really closer to 100 m, so you need to have a homogenous pixel over that scale to get an effective emissivity.
  • Sun position
