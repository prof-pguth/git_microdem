Stereo Net Options

Access by

  • By right clicking on the net.

  • From the Options, Geology/Geography tab.

Window controls appearance of stereo net, which will be redrawn after you accept "OK". Choices include:

  • Projection used, which can be Wulff (equal angle, Stereographic projection) or Schmidt (Equal area, Lambert Azimuthal equal area). 
  • Hemisphere used for projecting and plotting.  Lower hemisphere is standard for structural geology.
  • Legend
  • North tick
  • Center tick
  • Size of the net (small, medium, or large).
  • Grid to be drawn:  none, polar, or equatorial.
  • Grid size, or increment between grid lines of the polar and equatorial grids. 
  • Contouring colors options.
  • Contour counting radius
  • Contour counting radius
  • Grid color
