  • index grid in the Topography Viewer (https://geodata.md.gov/topoviewer/). When you open the topography viewer, look in the layers and tools on the right hand side about half way down you will see a Bulk Downloads option, check that box and the grid will display. Click in one of the cells and a pop up box will display showing you the block number. It will also provide an option to download that block, which tends to be very large.
