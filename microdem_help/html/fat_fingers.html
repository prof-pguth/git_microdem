Fat Finger Exercise

 

This shows you how good your mouse pointing is.  It is particulary relevant for those who want to use the trackpad on a laptop, and and restricting with accurcay of wielding their "fat finger".

Start two ways:

  • File: Introductory tutorials from the main screen, which will open a vector map.
  • File, Fat fingers from any map, which will use the current map

The target will appear near the center of the screen.

You can change the color of the target, to improve contrast on the map.

After each selection, you will see:

  • How far off the selection was, in the x and y directions
  • A grid showing your target shot group
  • A score (no partial credit)
  • The average error in pixels
  • The error in meters, given the current map scale
