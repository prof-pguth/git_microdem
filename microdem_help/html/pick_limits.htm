Pick DEM Grid Limits

Select the limits for display or analysis of a DEM grid, in terms of the grid postings, whose origin is in the SW corner, or the actual coordinates.

The default is for the entire DEM.

Interior of the DEM leaves a margin around the edges, which is appropriate when doing area computations which require a buffer around the points and the edges might affect the results.

Option on:

  • Modify Map Area popup, invoked by Modify menu choice on Map child window or by right click on the map window.
  • File , Save DEM, Pick corners, MD DEM
