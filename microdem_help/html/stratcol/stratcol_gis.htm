Stratcol GIS

Open Stratcol.

Go to options, and pick "Create GIS DB".  The column files must be in c:\mapdata\stratcol, and can be in subdirectories.

Open  base map.

Database  Open the stratcol database, c:\mapdata\stratcol\stratcol.dbf.  

You can open columns by using the Stratcol button on the map toolbar, and then double clicking on the map, or by double clicking on the record in the database table.
