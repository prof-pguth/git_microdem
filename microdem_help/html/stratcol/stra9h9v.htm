STRATCOL (not updated recently) Fossil Files

Files with fossil ranges are ASCII text files with the extension *.FO*. They look like this:

      8.9916         9.2964   Bayfieldia sp.A
      8.9916        21.5189   Bowmania sp.
      8.9916        22.6162   Euptychaspis typicalis
      8.9916        20.1473   Prosaukia sp.
     20.1473        20.4521   Bowmania n.sp. A
     20.1473        29.2608   Eurekia n.sp.A
     20.1473        22.6162   Illaenurus sp.
     21.5189        21.8237   Saukiella sp.
     21.5189        21.8237   Saukia sp.

There is one range per line. The first number is the base of the range, in meters from the base of the section, and the second number is the top of the range. If the name is enclosed in quotation marks, the entire name will be printed in italics.
