MICRODEM

From PETMAR Trilobite Breeding Ranch

MICRODEM has many options, some of which you may not need. You may turn off the least used options on the Menu settings button of the Program tab of the options form. In my opinion, all options are related, and putting them into one program eliminates the overhead of many small individual programs. I am a geologist and an educator, and that flavors my interpretation of what should be in the program. The options present are here for one of the following reasons:

  • Someone funded me to incorporate them.
  • They were necessary or useful for my research into the characteristics and geologic applications of DEMs.
  • They helped in one of the courses I teach.
  • They were fun.

I will consider adding other functions, but they must fit one of these categories, and the higher up on the list, the more likely I will do the work.
