FFT Output

The FFT option produces three output windows:

  1. Graph of the original function, which can be resized or rescaled.
  2. Graph of the power spectrum density, which can be resized or rescaled. You can also reprocess the FFT and vary some of the processing parameters. Reprocessing will redraw this graph and produce a new text window.
  3. Text window with the results. The harmonics will be sorted by power from most powerful to least powerful.
