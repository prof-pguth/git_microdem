Copy Coordinates for Registration

To get image coordinates, right click on the image window and pick "Copy image coordinates to clipboard"
To get ground coordinates (Lat/Long),
  • double click on record in gazetteer or other database, and pick "Copy point coordinates to clipboard".
  • right click on any registered map, and picking "Copy image coordinates to clipboard" .
Double click on the record to update in the registration database, or select "Next Point" to add a new one.

Insert either the lat/long (and POINT name) or the image coordinates.
