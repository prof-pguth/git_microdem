Other 1" DEMs:

  • "USGS 1 arc second DEM" (https://portal.opentopography.org/raster?opentopoID=OTNED.012021.4269.2 ) which is a terrible name because it includes data for Mexico and Canada
  • TanDEM-X 30m Edited DEM (EDEM)
  • TanDEM-X DEM Change: read the caveats about snow and vegetation and what time period is covered

    • https://geoservice.dlr.de/web/dataguide/tdm30/
  • DTMs (reprocessing of one or more DEMs; same concept as MERIT)
    • Hengl, T., Leal Parente, L., Krizan, J., & Bonannella, C. (2020). Continental Europe Digital Terrain Model at 30 m resolution based on GEDI, ICESat-2, AW3D, GLO-30, EUDEM, MERIT DEM and background layers (v0.3) [Data set]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.4724549
      • Integer resolution, in decimeters.  This will not work in areas with elevations above 3200 m
      • EGM2008, using the 5 arc degree correction surface
      • EPSG 3035 (LAEA)
      • Terrain derivatives https://zenodo.org/record/4495449#.YgUhvOrMLAQ
    • FABDEM (Forest And Buildings removed Copernicus DEM):  
      • Data source, version 2: https://data.bris.ac.uk/data/dataset/s5hqmjcdj8yo2ibzi9b4ew3sn
      • Journal Paper:  Hawker, Laurence and Uhe, Peter and Paulo, Luntadila and Sosa, Jeison and Savage, James and Sampson, Christopher and Neal, Jeffrey, 2022,A 30m global map of elevation with forests and buildings removed, Environmental Research Letters, 17 024016.  https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.1088/1748-9326/ac4d4f   
      • The DEM is listed as "pixel-is-area", but with a half pixel offset to match the Copernicus "pixel-is-point"
      • The DEM is everywhere 1" longitude spacing
      • https://samapriya.github.io/awesome-gee-community-datasets/projects/fabdem/
    • EDTM
    • DiluviumDEM:
      • Journal paper: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.rse.2023.113812 
      • Up to 80 m elevation
      • Dominick Dusseau, Zachary Zobel, Christopher R. Schwalm, 2023, DiluviumDEM: Enhanced accuracy in global coastal digital elevation models: Remote Sensing of Environment, Volume 298, https://doi.org/10.1016/j.rse.2023.113812.
      • Data download: https://zenodo.org/records/8384665
      • There was apparently a revised version of the data set posted, but Zenodo does not indicate it. Zenodo says Version 1.0 was posted 9/12/2023.  The unzipped TIF files in the first version may be dated 9/7/2003 (probably downloaded 1/29/2024), while the second may be dated 9/26/2023 (downloaded 4/10/2024).  The file names do not indicate a version.
      • The download all does not work; Edge reports a virus, while Chrome and FireFox just fail.  The download of indiviudal 5x5 degree blocks works
      • The data set has bad DEMs with 1-2 kb file size and incorrect Geotiff tags, which have problems in WBT, QGIS as well as MICRODEM.  A graphical directory view shows tiny rectangles or horizontal or vertical strips instead of a large square thumbnail.  There are approximately 277 such bad files, and 2717 good files
      • Tiles have variable number of rows and columns, from 3600 to 3602, which does not happen in any other the 1 degree data sets.
    • DeltaDTM
      • As of April 2025, there is a version 4 that goes up to 30 m elevation (original was 10 m)
      • Pronk, M., Hooijer, A., Eilander, D. et al. DeltaDTM: A global coastal digital terrain model. Sci Data 11, 273 (2024). https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-024-03091-9
      • Pronk, Maarten (2024): DeltaDTM: A global coastal digital terrain model. Version 2. 4TU.ResearchData. dataset. https://doi.org/10.4121/21997565.v2
      • https://data.4tu.nl/datasets/1da2e70f-6c4d-4b03-86bd-b53e789cc629 
    • CoastalDEM v3
      • https://go.climatecentral.org/coastaldem
      • 1" (licensed) and 3" (researchers can request) version
      • Up to 120 m elevation, even inland, and includes the entire tile if part is below 120 m
      • Pixel shift, so it matches the ALOS grid
      • Based on CopDEM, FABDEM, and DiluviumDTM
      • Kolp and Strauss, 2024, CoastalDEM v3.0: Improving fully global coastal elevation predictions through a convolutional neural network and multi-source DEM fusion  https://24975331.fs1.hubspotusercontent-eu1.net/hubfs/24975331/CoastalDEM_3___Scientific_White_Paper.pdf , Climate Central Scientific Report, 21 p.

      • 3 arc sec
      • non-commercial license
