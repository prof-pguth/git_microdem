Introductory Tutorials

On the File menu, Introductory tutorials:

These will download and open data, and provide a quick introduction to the uses of different types of data and the program's capabilities.
  • Annapolis GIS data sampler (slow, 2 GB download the first time run)
  • Annapolis TM8 scene satellite imagery (slow, 1 GB download the first time run)
  • Annapolis Lidar sampler (same slow, 8 GB download as the Annapolis data sampler the first time run)
  • Lidar sampler (350 MB download)
  • Harper's Ferry Terrain Analysis (150 MB download)
  • Annapolis redistricting
  • Grays scale: see how good your grayscale and color perception are.
  • RGB separates: enter a photograph, and the program will show each of the primary colors, in grayscale, and in that color
  • Nyquist: understand the Nyquist sampling theorem
  • Fat fingers
