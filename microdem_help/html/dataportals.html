Data Portals

  • National Map wide variety of data including DEMs and NLCD.
  • TIGER--roads and streams--overview.  Download TIGER data.   
  • USDA Portal: aerial imagery, topos, DEMS.  May get better results using an ftp program rather than  internet browser.
  • Coastal
  • DEM discovery portal--NGDC  
  • Hazards Data Download, USGS: http://hddsexplorer.usgs.gov/
  • Natural Earth Data
  • Africa, from Center for Geographic Analysis, Harvard University
  • store.usgs.gov: includes a side variety of data, including NGA JOGs (1:250K) in GeoPDF.  On the order form, click on the map to get the GeoPDF into the browser window.
  • USGS NHD--National Hydrography Dataset
  • Global Multi-Resolution Topography Data Portal  "Best" merge of marine (down to 100 m) and land data
  • U.S. Interagency Elevation Inventory:  http://www.csc.noaa.gov/inventory/#  (download as Geodatabse)
  • USDA NAIP: https://naip-usdaonline.hub.arcgis.com/
  • Fish and Wildlife Service
  • KML USGS 7.5' Quads
