Cut/Fill along Road

  • Open DEM .  The resolution and quality of the DEM will affect your results.
  • Digitize centerline of road, with several options.  If you use a Points file, the points must be in sequential order along the line.  You want to digitize zoomed in as much as possible; your accuracy picked a point is half the pixel size on screen at best, and worse if your hand/eye coordination is not perfect.
    • Annotate Map, Create shapefile, Points or Line.
    • Digitize in Google Earth, and create a points KML file, which you can open Database. If you want to create a line, you will have to convert to shapefile with DNRGPS
  • If you have a points shapefile, turn it into area/line shape file from points.
  • Create points file (along line) and pick a uniform spacing such as the grid spacing in the DEM
  • Verify the point spacing with Edit,   Navigation,  Add travel path (Azimuth, distance).
  • You can get a profile of the road centerline, with Stats: 2D graph: 2D graph, simple (points) with cumulative distance versus elevation.
  • Edit, DEM, Add interpolated elevation from DEM.
