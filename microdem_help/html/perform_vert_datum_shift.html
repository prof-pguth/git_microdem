Perform Vertical Datum Shifts

Requires the files for the geoid, get on the Geology/Geography tab of the options form.

Sub-Options on Edit  menu

  • WGS84 elllipsoid to EGM2008
  • EGM2008 to WGS84 ellipsoid
  • EGM96 to EGM2008
