Arbitrary DEM Merge

The merge operations are designed to merge a number of DEMs that belong to the same series, with similar characteristics.

If you want to merge any set of DEMs:

  • New DEM  that covers the entire area you want.
  • Subset & zoom to cover just the area you want (covering a little too much area is better than too little)
  • File menu, Save DEM, MICRODEM subset
  • Close the DEM, and open the subset.
  • File, Save DEM, reinterpolate.  Pick whether you want lat/long or UTM, and select the spacing you want.
  • Close the DEM, and open the reinterpolated DEM.
  • Edit, Mark as missing the entire DEM.
  • Edit, DEM holes to put DEMs into the merge.  The order is important; some options will use the last value, and some will only replace values for holes.

This option will not do any checking that your merge makes sense (it will happily interpolate ETOPO5 down to 1 m resolution), and does nothing about edge matching, so consider the old adage GIGO and take full credit/blame for the resulting DEM.
