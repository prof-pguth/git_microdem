MICRODEM on Big Screens

The Nile Delta from Landsat on three monitors.  Each shows the full resolution of the 7000x7000 pixel scene (25% reduction to fit on the page, and to keep the file size down).  Note that the laptop only shows a postage stamp area compared to even the 2K monitor, and hardly anything compared to the 4K monitor.

 

 

Four monitors, each 1920x1200.  See detail and context at the same time.  Despite my early reservations, the brain easily ignored the gaps between monitors.

Or just get a 4K monitor or TV, which shows the same number of pixels.
