GIS Dissolve

The dissolve operation takes many small vector records, and combines them in larger, connected segments.  Often road networks or coastlines will be composed of many small segments, and operations may work better if they are merged.

Options to dissolve lines (like Tiger roads or coastlines)
  • Use QGIS
    • Open QGIS
    • Open vector layer
    • Vector, geoprocessing, dissolve
    • Save the resulting shapefile for use in MICRODEM
