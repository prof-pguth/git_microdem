Other software:

  • Cloud Compare, with CSF plugin
    • https://www.cloudcompare.org/doc/wiki/index.php?title=CSF_(plugin)
    • Zhang W, Qi J, Wan P, Wang H, Xie D, Wang X, Yan G. An Easy-to-Use Airborne LiDAR Data Filtering Method Based on Cloth Simulation. Remote Sensing. 2016; 8(6):501.
    • Creates ground points, off ground points, and a ground mesh.
    • Only works with the GUI, so one tile at at time
    • DLL and EXE at http://ramm.bnu.edu.cn/projects/CSF/download/
    • http://ramm.bnu.edu.cn/researchers/wumingzhang/english/default_contributions.htm
    • 2003 ISPRS report on ground filtering
    • CSFDemo_v2
      • Needs ASCII input, no header line, XYZ with space or comma separation
      • Output will be ASCII, tab separation, in a different order
    • There are some experimental options for this on the Experimental tab of the data manipultion form.  With most lidar coming with ground classification, and other options list above, this one is harder to implement.
