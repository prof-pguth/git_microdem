Plate Boundary Shapefiles

Button on map window toolbar

Plate boundaries are color coded:

  • Red for trenches
  • Green for ridges (includes small transform segments)
  • Orange for transform/strike-slip boundaries.


There are a number of plate boundary files on the internet.  Most are very small scale, and when you zoom in to complex regions like the Mediterrean sea, the lines often are not complete.  They also typically code all the tranform segments on the ridges the same as the spreading centers, which is perhaps a matter of semantics.

Plate boundaries are vector line files.
