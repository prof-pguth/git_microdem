Register Historic Map scans

These maps will not have a designated projection, which may be very hard to determine, and may not be scanned or corrected to have the edges of the map parallel with the edges of the scan.  They also do not have a clear datum.  For a first approximation, at this scale, the datum may not make a difference.  The final results should be checked, and if necessary, a datum correction applied.
