3D Map Reflectance Merge

This product combines shading from the DEM and the map.

With the DEM and map open, right click on the map and select "Display parameter" and then "DEM reflectance merge".  You can adjust the lighting with a right click and "Reflectance options"; both the sun position and the vertical exaggeration will affect the shading. 

There are several possible ways to do hillshading.

Base map.
Reflectance merge
Reflectance merge.  Less vertical exaggeration for reflectance parameters
DRG anaglyph--applies to other maps as well
