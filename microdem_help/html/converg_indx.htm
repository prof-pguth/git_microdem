Convergence Index

The convergence index computes ridges and valleys.  We are not adjusting for the diagonal angular spacing of arc second DEMs not being exactly 45 degrees.

Accessible References:

  • Thommeret, Bailly and Puech (Geomorphometry 2009)
  • Kiss, R., 2004, Determination of drainage network in digital elevation model, utilities and limitations Journal of Hungarian Geomathematics, vol.2, p.16-29.

Original reference in German, 1993 and 1994 papers by Köthe and Lehmeier, SARA—ein system zur automatischen relief-analyse.
