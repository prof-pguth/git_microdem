Quick Profile Exploration

If you have a database with a lot of magnetic profiles, you can rapidly view them:

  • Double click on  a profile in the database table form.
  • The profile will be highlighted on the map.
  • Size the record display window so you can also see the map. The profile will resize when you pick another record, or you can right click and force it to redraw.
  • Cycle through the profiles with the <Prev and >Next buttons.
