Triple Junction Bathymetry

  This map shows the Smith & Sandwell predicted topography around the Indian Ocean triple junction with the interpreted plate boundaries in red.

Note the NE-SW pattern of fracture zones radiating from the NW and SE ridges, and the N-S pattern on the western portion of the SW ridge. On the portion of the SW ridge adjacent to the triple junction, however, note the absence of fracture zone perpendicular to the ridge.

Additional analysis of this triple junction:

  • Based on predicted sea floor ages.
  • Based on modeled plate velocities.
  • Based on identification of magnetic anomalies.
