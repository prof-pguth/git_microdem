Plate Motion Calculators

 

Available if you do not want to use MICRODEM, or if you want to verify results.

  • Complex, text based: http://www.unavco.org/software/geodetic-utilities/plate-motion-calculator/plate-motion-calculator.html.  Discusses a number of models
  • http://ofgs.aori.u-tokyo.ac.jp/~okino/platecalc_new.html
  • http://geoscience.wisc.edu/~chuck/MORVEL/motionframe_mrvl.html  can do up to 1000 points, with 25 plates from MORVEL
  • http://geoscience.wisc.edu/~chuck/MORVEL/motionframe_nnrm56.html can do up to 1000 points, with 56 plates from NNR-MORVEL
