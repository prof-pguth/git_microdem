Map Shading Options

Accessed on the Modify menu, or from the pop up menu when right clicking on the map.

DEM Color merge let you put colors from various layers together.  This is most effective for combining topography with satellite imagery or especially a scanned map

Elevation can come from a separate open DEM.

Grayscale and subdue emphasize overlays instead of the base map.
