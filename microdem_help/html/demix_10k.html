DEMIX 10K Tiles

This is a system of nominally 10 km tiles covering the entire world.  The actual spacing is in arc minutes, and each 1x1 degree tile on earth is divided into 10-100 subtiles

The letters used for the subtiles exclude N/S and E/W because those are used for the 1 degree tile names, and I/O because they can be confused for 1/0 
  • LongLetters  = ('A','B','C','D','F','G','H','J','K','L') and increase from west to east
  • LatLetters = ('M','P','Q','R','T','U','V','X','Y','Z') and increase from south to north
Tile Naming: [N,S]yy[LatLetter] [W,E]xxx[LongLetter]
