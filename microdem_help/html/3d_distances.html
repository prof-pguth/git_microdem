3D Measures of Distance

 

Not "significant" until you get to very steep slopes.

The walking distance on the slope will be more than the horizontally projected distance, but you have to be at significant slopes befores it really has much effect.  And if you really want to be precise, your horizontal path will not be straight, but wander about, and the steeper the terrain, the more of that there will be.  Anyone planning cross country hiking will take both of those into account (based on the contour lines if using an old fashioned map), and figure that into their trip planning.
