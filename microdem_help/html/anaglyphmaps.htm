Anaglyph Maps

These maps require red/blue glasses.

Sources for glasses.

Stereo Anaglyph: creates a new reflectance map with a shift of the red bit plane. You can change the maximum shift, as well as the reflectance parameters, by right clicking on the map display.
Stereo Anaglyph/Merge: uses the image on screen, and shifts the red bit plane to the left. This may or may not be effective depending on the imagery displayed. You will be asked for a maximum shift. This is a Landsat TM image.
