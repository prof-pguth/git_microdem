Copy to Clipboard

From the clipboard move to Paint, Word, or PowerPoint.

Clipboard export is usually available:

  • On File menu.
  • By right clicking
  • On map window toolbar


You will then get a popup window

  • "Quick display" on the left shows just the map as it appeared on screen.  This maximizes the information on the map.  There is an option to put a frame around the map.
  • "Detailed map: display on the right provides a more formal display with the lat/long graticule.

Print,  prepare  printer image allows more deliberate control on the map.

Scalebar: Legends/marginalia after right clicking on the map, lets you put scalebar on map.  This helps with your analysis, and helps your readers.
