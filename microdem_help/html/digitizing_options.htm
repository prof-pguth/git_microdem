Digitizing Options

Digitizing can be in two modes:

  • Point--each point is selected only by double clicking.  In point mode, you end selection by right clicking.
  • Stream--points are selected automatically as you move the mouse.  With stream digitizing you set a tolerance, and points will be added only when you move the specified number of pixels in either the x or y direction. A large tolerance leads to fewer points, while a small tolerance leads to smoother curves.

In both modes, for options that require an enclosed polygon, the program will automatically close the area.

Digitizing mode is set on Printer/Hardware tab of options form. You can also change mode by right clicking on the map while digitizing.

The "better" selection mode depends on the project, and personal preferences (hand-eye coordination varies among people).  Digitization will probably be best with a mouse, and not the trackpad on a laptop.

These same modes apply in Google Earth and other programs.
