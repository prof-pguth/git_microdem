Rules for dBase Field Names

  • Labels can only use 'A' to 'Z', '0' to '9', and '_', and  must be 10 characters or less in length, all caps.
  • The first character should be a letter.
  • The last character of the field name should be a letter rather than a number.  Violation of this is likely to lead to problems if you want to filter the database.
  • Each field  in the database must have a unique name.
