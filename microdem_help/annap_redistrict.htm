Annapolis Redistricting

Go to File, GIS Labs, Annapolis redistricting.   It has the following components:

  • TIGER Edges for Anne Arundel County
  • Shapefile with  Annapolis city limits
  • Results by block for the 2010 census, for the current area of the city of Annapolis.  This is the main shapefile you will use.
  • Results by block for the 2000 census, for the current area of the city of Annapolis (it includes areas annexed to the city after after the 2000 census).  This is supplied for historical context, since one interesting question is how the city is growing, and where the increase in the Hispanic population is taking place.
