Selection map at http://openaltimetry.org/.

  • Tracks in red, with the selected track in green.
  • Clicking on a track shows the available dates, quarterly.
  • Picking a date shows the lidar beams in colored dots for the 6 beams, in three pairs.  The 6 beams, in pairs 90 m apart, with strong and weak beams, and 3.3 km from the next pair of beams.
  • Some dates will have no data or partial data, depending on weather.
